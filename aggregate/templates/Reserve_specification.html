The objective and market values track from . The Difference column is the difference between Market and Objective, which corresponds to the risk margin. Equity increases from statutory to objective because of limited liability, but decreases to market because of risk margins. The latter are far more material than the former for a well capitalized company, so there is a more material difference between Market and Objective equity than between Objective and Statutory.
